About Me
As a developer, my primary focus is on maintaining a stable livelihood, ensuring that I can support myself through my work while also finding personal fulfillment. Coding forms the foundation of my professional life—it's what keeps the lights on, quite literally. But beyond the day-to-day of programming, which can often be technical and logical, I have a deep love for my girl Thet Hter Swe and creativity, which I channel through freelance art. Freelancing offers me the flexibility to work on different artistic projects that challenge me in ways coding doesn’t. Whether I’m working on digital illustrations, visual design, or something more experimental, art serves as a way to explore self-expression and creativity in a way that contrasts the structure of development work. It’s also a way for me to merge my interests, as the world of design and code increasingly overlap in fields like web development and UX/UI design. In addition to my work in development and art, I’m a committed student, constantly striving to excel academically. My studies give me a broader perspective and allow me to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in technology and creativity. Education isn’t just a means to an end for me—it’s an essential part of my growth, both personally and professionally. Being a student adds structure and purpose to my daily routine, and it pushes me to be disciplined, organized, and always hungry for knowledge. It’s a crucial element that keeps my mind sharp and opens up new opportunities in both coding and freelance art. Balancing these three distinct areas—coding, freelance art, and academic excellence—keeps my life incredibly dynamic and multifaceted. Each day is different. One day, I might be knee-deep in complex code, working to solve a technical problem for a client, and the next, I’m sketching out ideas for an art project, brainstorming creative concepts, or studying for an exam. This constant juggling of roles means that my life never feels stagnant or dull. There’s always something new to learn, create, or achieve, and it all works together to keep me engaged and excited about the future. At the end of the day, this balance ensures that I always have something meaningful on my plate, literally and figuratively, as I continue to pursue my passions while making a living and advancing my education.
